Welcome to Masjid Adam

Masjid Adam(A New Light, A New Home) is a project of MUNA Center Of Paterson. This is the first Masjid in the Bangladeshi Muslim community in Paterson (NJ) that have a prayer space for our sisters and the Masjid dedicated to helping our youth build a strong sense of Muslim identity. This Masjid is the new platform for us to continue our work in serving the Muslims and humanity at large.

    The Five Pillars of Islam
  • 1. Faith or belief in the Oneness of God (Allah) and the finality of the Prophethood of Muhammad, peace be upon him (pbuh);
  • 2. Establishment of the 5 daily prayers;
  • 3. Concern for and almsgiving to the needy;
  • 4. Self-purification through fasting in the month of Ramadan.
  • 5. The pilgrimage to Mecca for those who are able.

Masjid Adam Services

Our Regular services

5 Daily Prayers

We offer five daily mandatory muslim prayer. Our Masjid is open for all

Islamic Education

We offer weekend ans summer kids islamic school. Adult quran learning program.

Jummah Prayer

Muslims gather for congregational worship during the Friday midday prayer.

Youth Program

We have youth program for boys and girls. We do regular halaqah, Quran teaching, Basic Islamic Knowladge

Community Service

We have food drive program for homeless people. We do community service such as cleaning, immigration help, charity, workshops, job search
